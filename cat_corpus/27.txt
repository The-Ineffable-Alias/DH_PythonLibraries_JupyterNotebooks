Product description
Feline Pet-Ema is a disposable enema that treats constipated cats with a safe and effective syringe dispenser. Cats that suffer from constipation can be a symptom of a larger problem- and it's important to treat the underlying cause. However, this problem can create urgency that needs individual attention. This enema works to soften your cat's stool and ease this symptom while your cat waits to be treated for the root problem.
Directions & Dosage
Remove the cap from the appropriate size syringe.Lubricate the nozzle, if desired, and then gently insert it into your pet's rectum.Depress the plunger to move the product from the nozzle into your pet's rectum.Repeat in one hour if instructed to do so by your veterinarian.
Ingredients
Each syringe of Pet-Ema for Cats contains 6 mL of a solution of Dioctyl Sodium Sulfosuccinate 125 mg in Glycerin, with Sorbic Acid (as a preservative). Each syringe of Pet-Ema for Dogs contains 12 mL of a solution of Dioctyl Sodium Sulfosuccinate 250 mg in Glycerin, with Sorbic Acid (as a preservative). 685687219343-3
